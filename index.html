Welcome to ABU SHAWAYA – Where Flavor Rules the Fire!

At ABU SHAWAYA, we believe great food starts with great passion. Born from a love for bold spices, tender meat, and perfectly crispy textures, our restaurant brings you the ultimate broast and grilled chicken experience in Bangalore.

Whether you’re craving the juicy crunch of our signature broast chicken or the smoky richness of our perfectly grilled chicken, every bite at ABU SHAWAYA is a journey of flavor. Our secret marinades, time-honored techniques, and fire-grilled perfection are what set us apart.

More than just a meal, ABU SHAWAYA is a celebration of taste, tradition, and togetherness. We serve with heart, we cook with soul—and we welcome you to be a part of our story.

Pull up a chair, dig in, and let the flavor speak for itself.
